Illustrations

I have the most fun drawing origninal characters. May that be of my friends or my very own. Character drawings, or just drawings in general, is my go to creativity dump. I just love how drawing can be used in different ways to express differen things, may that be an advocacy, a belief, an emotion, a feeling, or a societal stance. You could say that drawing for me is a window to my concious and the subconcious of myself.
